Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 5, 2026

Institution Name: University Of Skills Training And Entrepreneurial Development (USTED)

System Name: LibPass Visitor Management System

Compliance Standard: Ghana Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843)

This Privacy Policy explains how USTED collects, uses, stores and protects personal and biometric data through the LibPass Library Visitor Management System, in compliance with Ghana's Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843).

We collect the following personal data during registration:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Gender
  • Department
  • Institutional ID number (students and staff)
  • Email address (students and staff)
  • Academic level (students only)
  • Employment status (staff only)
  • Facial biometric descriptor (a mathematical representation of your face, not a photograph)

Data is collected solely for:

  • Library access management and visitor identification
  • Accurate counting and reporting of library visits
  • Generation of a unique library registration ID and QR code

Your facial data is stored as a 128-point mathematical descriptor, not as a photograph or image. This descriptor cannot be reverse-engineered into a recognizable image of your face. It is used exclusively for identity verification at the library entrance.

All data is stored securely on encrypted cloud servers. Access to the system is protected by PIN-based authentication. Only authorized library staff can access visitor records.

  • Student records are retained until graduation and deactivated thereafter
  • Staff records are retained until departure from the institution
  • Guest records are retained indefinitely for historical visit data
  • Visit logs are retained for reporting and statistical purposes

Under the Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data held by the institution
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by contacting the library administration
